ShaadiMe is a full-service wedding planning partner — not a vendor marketplace. We don't show you a list of vendors to call. Instead, we assign you a dedicated planner who understands your vision, coordinates with every vendor, and ensures your wedding day goes exactly as you imagined. You plan once. We handle everything.
Our planning fee depends on the scale and complexity of your wedding. We work with budgets starting from ₹5 Lakhs. When you start planning, your ShaadiMe planner will share a transparent breakdown before anything is confirmed. No surprises, no hidden fees.
We are currently live in Bengaluru, Chennai, and Hyderabad. These are our launch cities. We are expanding soon — if you are in another city, fill in the form and we will reach out when we launch there.
The earlier the better — especially for large weddings. Ideally, we love to start at least 6 months before the wedding date. That said, we have worked magic with shorter timelines too. Reach out and we will tell you honestly what we can do.
We can work with that. Whether you need us for just the décor, the catering, or full ceremony management — we shape our involvement around you. Start planning and your planner will customise everything to fit your needs.
